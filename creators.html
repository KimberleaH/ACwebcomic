The Creators

In the beginning, there was nothingness: born of chaos, ever swirling, and order, calmly flowing. Together, the creators offer stability. Eternally, they maintain the balance of the universe.

Kimberlea and Samuel live in Kansas and met in early grade school. The two love stories in all their forms. They have both dedicated the last seventeen years to the story that matters the most: Ancient Chronicles.

Kimberlea Heili

Creator of Chaos

Kim, Whisperspirit

she/they

Kimberlea is a passionate storyteller addicted to world building, and holds a strong appreciation for science, nature, and birds. She studied animation and game art design and occasionally freelanced as a concept artist. Kimberlea is the illustrator for official artwork, concepts, character designs, and comic pages. She spends much of her time learning new things, writing, playing with her pigeons, tending to her fish, and raiding unsuspecting pantries.

Samuel Page

Creator of Order

Sam, Sammy, Quilan

he/him

Samuel is an avid world builder and storyteller. He has taken classes on photography and art, and spends many of his hours immersed in research in an effort to expand the believability of his work. Samuel assists the production of comics as a lightning-fast colorist, and is especially fond of developing cultures, governments, and political systems. He has a deep love for magpies, crows, ravens, and other corvids.

The story came from humble beginnings, and was first started in 2007. Coming up with characters and crazy ideas for “the greatest story ever” was a favorite pastime throughout our childhood. It was easy to get carried away. We'd come up with the skeletons of five or so books, had grand plans for a second series, and amassed enough characters to create an army. But as the story developed, we started reeling things in.
