Vezdra

By Kimberlea Heili

August 10, 2022

Vezdra, at one time, were supposed to look like and be related to sefora (as seen below). But their appearance wasn’t apt for their behavior and made it awkward calling them fauna. This new design was actually spurred by a dream I had about a creature of similar form. Everything about vezdra at the time prior was pretty hardcore and the original design has never quite fit their nature. Now, however, the new look checks all the boxes and is so much more badass. Very little has changed about them since the revamp. Electrolocation and the deterioration of wings with age were new additions.
