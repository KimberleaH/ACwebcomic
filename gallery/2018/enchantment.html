Enchantment

By Kimberlea Heili

2018

Practicing some dynamic posing and showing off Leall’s unique ability. He used to be in with an assassin guild although not by choice. An experimental mekhos was surgically implanted into his back. It draws power from his kor to fuel ethereal but functional wings and sets his shortswords ablaze at will. These drain him of energy and can only be used for a limited time. In present day, Leall is no longer in the guild and has sought medical advice to remove the mekhos but the risks seemed to great since the device is heavily integrated with his kor. To remove it would mean causing more damage than when it had been put in.
