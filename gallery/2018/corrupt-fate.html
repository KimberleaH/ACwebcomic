Corrupt – Fate

By Kimberlea Heili

May 8, 2018

Long story short, the Corrupt — a name given to them by the people — are celestial beings of raw corrupted, negative energy. Some believe that long, long ago there was a time when they weren’t corrupted at all. Other’s believe they’re monsters spawned by Korsin, a great evil of the past. The Corrupt died out long ago however.
