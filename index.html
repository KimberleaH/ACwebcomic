News

June 12, 2021

Making Progress

Hi folks! We're gradually making headway on our efforts to return from hiatus. Chapter 1 itself is about 80% fully inked so here's the plan thus far:
(1) finish updating the cast's color references
(2) color the 80% of pages that are ready for color
(3) finish inking and coloring the last 20%
And once Chapter 1 is finished, we'll announce a return date, whenever that'll be. We're just two nerds working on this project in our spare time, so we appreciate your patience and support. If you'd like to support us more directly, we are currently accepting donations. Come join us on Discord and spread the word about our story!


December, 11 2020

Accessibility and Responsive Update

Hello lovelies and gentle-folk! 'Tis the Kimberlea. The website is finally up to snuff! I've recently added an accessibility update and the site is now mobile friendly! If you experience any bugs, please get in touch with us on social media or email us directly, and I'll sort out the problem the best I can. At this time, I'll be touching up the transcripts and adding one more accessibility feature in the near future.


May, 23 2020

New Website - On Hiatus

Hello folks! Our brand new website has arrived! Be sure to check here for noteworthy news on Ancient Chronicles. Follow us on social media or join our discord to keep up on updates like new articles and content. Click on Lore to go to our World Anvil page and dive into the juicy world building that as gone into the development of the Universe. A good place to start would be the featured articles below.

Comic pages are currently on hiatus but will be returning soon. In the meantime, we're continuing to world build, create concept art, and write articles for World Anvil, so be sure to keep an eye out for the next additions.
